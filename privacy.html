Slackwarrior Privacy Policy

What we collect

Slackwarrior collects information that is sent directly to him, via direct message or when he is mentioned. He also saves your inthe.am API Key to get access to your task list. Please keep in mind that inthe.am keeps a copy of your tasks.

Analytics services from dashbot.io are used to improve the Slackwarrior's services. During that process parts of your conversations with Slackwarrior are sent to their servers for analysis.

Slackwarrior also collects Slack account and access information from users for the purposes of connecting to the Slack API and to authenticate access to information on the Slackwarrior website.

Using standard web analytics and web server technologies, the Slackwarrior website logs your navigation actions, IP address, cookies and other information provided by your web browser.

How we use your data

Your data will only be used for the purpose of improving the bot and for finding/analyzing bugs. We will not give your data to any third party not mentioned here and we will not send you emails or otherwise bug you.
